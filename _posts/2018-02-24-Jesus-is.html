"The light shines in the darkness, and the darkness has not overcome it."
-John 1:5

Jesus is
Invigorating morning air in
The breath of a bird whistling to
Swirling mist condensing on
Blades of grass.
Golden rays breaking forest shade in
Warmth on the ground surrounding
Plants breaking boundaries of
Earth tinged with vitality in
The arrival of dawn.
Harmony immediate to
The peak of a mountain supported by
Shifting plates in restless communion with
The wanderer seeking
Creation's hidden valley.
Rolling waves of the ocean
Pushing forward to reach
A destination unseen yet palpably
Present in
The crashing of waves on
Opulent shores
Eternal.
